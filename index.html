About Me


Hi! I'm Vo Chi Lam, a second-year student majoring in Information Technology at Ho Chi Minh City University of Education.

I have a strong passion for programming, especially with C++ and algorithms. I've been actively participating in coding contests and personal projects to improve my problem-solving skills and logical thinking.

Recently, I've become more interested in Backend development, aiming to build efficient and reliable systems. I enjoy learning, practicing, and sharing knowledge every day.

I'm always open to connecting with like-minded people and growing together in the tech community!
